About Me

I'm a Product Manager with over 8 years of experience building products that matter. My journey spans across diverse industries—from AI Safety Tech, Primary Care and Insurance, Sports Technology, and IoT Telematics—but my approach remains consistent: crafting products through empathy, data, and meticulous attention to detail.

What sets me apart is my unique background as an amateur writer and poet. I see products as stories—narratives that connect with users on a human level while solving real problems. This perspective allows me to bridge the gap between technical complexity and user understanding, transforming intricate systems into intuitive experiences.

My expertise lies in translating complex technical requirements into clear, actionable product strategies. Whether it's developing AI safety protocols, streamlining healthcare workflows, enhancing sports performance through technology, or optimizing IoT telematics systems, I bring a human-centered approach to every challenge.

I believe that the best products emerge from understanding not just what users need, but why they need it. This empathy-driven approach, combined with rigorous data analysis and attention to detail, has consistently delivered products that users love and businesses value.
